Welcome to the Currency Tally Application

Efficient Management of Currency Denominations:
Experience a streamlined process for managing various currency denominations. This user-friendly application is designed to facilitate the easy input, update, and tracking of currency data.

Advanced Data Visualization:
Visualize your currency data effectively with integrated charting tools. Our application offers intuitive ways to analyze and understand your currency distributions, enhancing your data analysis experience.

Seamless Database Integration:
Connect effortlessly with a MySQL database for robust data storage and retrieval. This secure integration ensures that your currency data is stored safely and can be accessed anytime.

Excel Export Functionality:
Extend your data analysis with our Excel export feature. Export your currency data seamlessly to Excel, allowing for more detailed examination and reporting.
